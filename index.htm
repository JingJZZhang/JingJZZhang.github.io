Jing Zhang
Jing Zhang (张倞)

 

I'm a postdoc at Bar-Ilan University, hosted by Assaf Rinot, supported by the Israel Academy of Sciences and Humanities. I got my PhD from Carnegie Mellon University, under James Cummings. Before that, I studied in the National University of Singapore as an undergraduate. My research interests are in set theory, including infinite combinatorics, large cardinals, forcing theory etc. But my curiosity in general is broad.
